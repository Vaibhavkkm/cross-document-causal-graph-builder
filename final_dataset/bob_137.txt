
Author: Robert James Henderson

Location: CAIRO

Date: September 15,
      
3.45 M  FOUR MILLIÈMES 4 EGYPT POSTAGE  CARTE POSTALE Dear Jane. What do you think of our party on the back of this card We had a most interesting afternoon at the Pyramids with lots of fun with donkeys & camels & guides. etc Am enclosing a couple of clean post cards in letter Nana is writing.  Have been trying to find Reg Bull but have so far ^have not been successful He may have gone to front. This Cairo & surroundings are very wonderful & most fascinating  Will write soon  lots of Love Bob Miss Henderson. Raglan St. Drummoyne Sydney. N.S.W. Australia.
