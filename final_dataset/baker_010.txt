France   22/8/1916  Dear Mother  We are into it  again & by the time you  get this we will be out  again. All is well with   me & I am not going into  the front line this time. Am  staying in transport lines & make  one trip daily to battalion with  stores Etc.  We have pushed the Bosch  well back on this sector.  Have no real news for you  am in the "pink" of condition  Fondest love to All  God bless & keep you  Ever Your loving Son,  Ernie.
