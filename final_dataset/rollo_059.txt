Author: Harold Edwin Salisbury
Date: 10 November 1915
Nationality: Australian

Text:
  Have had to manufacture my own  Xmas Cards - shops close early here - but  I hope they reach you in time. Mail  goes in a few minutes so I must close  with love to you all - best wishes for a  Merry Xmas and for a happy prosperous  & successful New Year - & many of them  Rollo   Enclosed  Xmas Card from  ANZAC 1915  Medit. Exped. Force  Gallipoli  &  Field Card for Harry
