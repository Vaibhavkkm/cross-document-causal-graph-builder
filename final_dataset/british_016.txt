Author: Sidney Broad Hodges. 
Date: 30 December 1915.
Location: Longmoor, England. 
Regiment: Royal Engineers Railway Troop. 
Regiment number: 138032. 
Rank: Sapper. 
Nationality: British.

I might say we are all merry and bright and not down hearted yet.
Dear Mr Hunt,
Received card alright for which I must thank you very much. We are still at Longmoor and no signs of moving as I have been made cook’s mate and had to work very hard all through Xmas.
Sapper Hodges
Front of postcard:
I am sending you this
THE FIRST OR GRENADIER REGIMENT OF FOOT GUARDS
To Wish you A Pleasant Christmas
