Author: F M O’Donnell
Date: 14 November 1917
Nationality: Australian

Text:

3rd Australian Division Base Depot. 14.11.1917. General Sir W.R. Birdwood KCB. K.C.S.I. KCMG. CIE. DSO. Dear General Birdwood, Would you please accept my hearty congratulations on your promotion to rank of General which has appeared in the press today. May you be long spared to continue the good work and after triumphing over the enemy to come to our dear Australia to remain one of us until the end of the chapter. Yours truely F.M. O'Donnell
