Author: Wilfred Evans
Date: 12 April 1916
Regiment: 9th Light Horse 
Nationality: Australian

Banked to Wilfred.
These are the very beds I had charge of when at No I A.G.H between the 'X's. &  also inside on the same floor. Always had a spell for morning tea on that piazza.

6-The Sydney Mail, April 12, 1916.
 No. 1 Australian General Hospital, Heliopolis. Note the beds in the open and right round the piazza. This hospital was formerly known as the Palace Hotel.
