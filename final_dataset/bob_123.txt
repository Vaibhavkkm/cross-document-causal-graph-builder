Author: Robert James Henderson 

Location: France
 Date: 13.1.1918
 My dear Mum, Just received a lovely large mail letters dated Nov 5th Oct 29th Oct 22nd one from Lorna 29th Oct also three bosca parcels which were simply great also received a large tin of Creamy Chock biscuits from Snow Arrnott they were most acceptable Am enclosing some snaps of some of the Battn officers taken at our last camp in the forward area just behind the line about Xmas time some of them are quite good. have written some particulars on the back of them Am very busy tonight doing some work so shall have to defer answering your letters for the present Will have
another shot as soon as possible. Hope you receive these photos OK lots of love to all, Bob.
