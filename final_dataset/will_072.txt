Author: Walter H Long
Date: 13 March 1917
Location: Zepp
Nationality: British

Text:




COLONIAL OFFICE    
17.3. 1917  My dear General  My most hearty congratns  upon your being first into  Bapaume. A great  feat & one on which  the Australian troops  are to be heartily  congratulated. I felt  sure they meant to   get there very soon.  What an extraordinary  upheaval in Russia!  So far as one can judge  from Buchanan's telegrams  the change is all for  the good. This new
Gov. mean to persecute   the War with renewed  vigour so great good  ought to result.  An abortive Zepp  raid here & one of them  downed by the French.  Sincerl yours  Walter H. Long
