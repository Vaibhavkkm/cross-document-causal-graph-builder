Author: H Hastings Willis
Date: 26 January 1916
Location: Royal Alfred Hospital Camperdown Sydney
Nationality: Australian

Text:



ROYAL PRINCE ALFRED HOSPITAL, CAMPERDOWN, SYDNEY 26. 1. 1916  Dear Mrs Thomas Herewith I am enclosing the sum of Two pounds five shillings being coaching fees which I have collected from Drs. Rosenthal Cameron & Hill for Dr. Wilfred Evans. I understand that you are looking after his affairs while he is at the Front. I am writing to him and will let him know the details of the matter. Please acknowledge receipt. Yours faithfully, H. Hastings Willis M.B.a
