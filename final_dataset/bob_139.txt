
Date: 25.7.1917  This Space for communication   The address to be written here Dear Mick Sending you this photo of the old football XV at StJohns College. You will easily pick out Lee Scott & myself. Congrats on the L.L.B degree you will be some boy now. Had a glorious time in London did not live the true virtuous life I am afraid & very often did not see day light however such is life & you know old Lee Scott. Back once again with the old Battalion but certainly under different circumstances but gee whiz it breaks ones heart leaving Blightly Received a little crack in hand as soon as I got back but OK now best of luck, Len.
