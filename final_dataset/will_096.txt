
Author: W H Greenly
Date: 30 July 1917
Location: Sussex
Nationality: British

Text:

30.7.1917     My dear General - The very best of   thanks for your   charming letter.  Of course I am absolutely  delighted & the proudest  man in all the  world : I can only say
it was exceedingly fortunate for me that  you were not eligible!  in spite of technicalities   we always regard you  as a 12th Lancer & are  all very fond of the   connections.  I'll tell Fane how sorry  you were first to have   missed seeing them  but I hope post-ops  you'll be able to make   it good soon. So  far as we can tell  we may be about  this area some time  & should love to see 
you whenever you would  set a day to come  over.    Forever  WH Greenly
