Author: Frederick Harold Tubb.
Date: Monday 07.06.1915.
Nationality: Australian.

 Heard last night that Capt Finlayson had his wounded leg amputated last Saturday, (Extremely sorry! Flies are in myriads & very annoying, Major Tedder at mess last night who had just come out from Alexandria said that 17 troopships had been sent to the Dardanelles for more wounded, Achi Babi had not yet fallen. Evidently fierce fighting has been going on lately Our Bde has not yet gone to Lemnos fr as before stated. Received a message from O.room Sgt Wilkinson saying that Capt Finlayson was much improved - No news yet of my going!
