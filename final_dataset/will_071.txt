
Author: Walter H Long
Date: 14 March 1917
Location: Anzac
Nationality: British

Text:



COLONIAL OFFICE     My dear General.  I must send you a  line of warm thanks  for your personal kindness  & hospitality. I was  very greatly interested in,  & impressed by, all I saw  & came away fully  convinced that our Dominion troops are  well able to continue  to take the same
brilliant share of  this fresh struggle as  they have done from  for beginning.  I enjoyed my ride on  that charming horse  more than I can say &  was much struck by the  thoroughness of his  training & his fine   appearance of the waler
My very warm thanks.  Sincerely yours  Walter H Long  14. 3. 1917.
