Author: Grover Carter
Date: 31 March 1918
Rank: Lieutenant
Nationality: American

Text:

March 31, 1918 – Very windy but sun shining. No work all day. Damn tired of this life. A good case of the “Memphis Blues”. Have had no mail for over 3 weeks and I want to see Mother and Louise so very much. A very unhappy Easter Sunday. Wrote 5 letters.
