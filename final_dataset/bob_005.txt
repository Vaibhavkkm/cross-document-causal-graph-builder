To Mr Jas. Henderson At Sea 14/9/1915 Dear Sir I am pleased to inform you that your sons Offc LA & Cpl R.J. are is in the best of health and spirits. We have had a very pleasant voyage and tho' it is getting rather hot as we approach its termination, everyone is looking forward with interest to the new land we expect to reach in a day or two.
HL Henley Lieut O/C 7th Run 13th Brd.
