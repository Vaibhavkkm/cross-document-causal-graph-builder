Author: Grover Carter
Date: 24 April 1918
Rank: Lieutenant
Nationality: American

Text:

April 24, 1918 – Very intense shelling by us for about four hrs. last night. Heaviest firing I have seen, 7-11 p.m. Nothing doing during the day. At night, 11:30 o’clock, left for new camp. Stopped for the night about 1½ miles north of Abeele.
