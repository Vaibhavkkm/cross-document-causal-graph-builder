Author: Grover Carter
Date: 6 May 1918
Rank: Lieutenant
Nationality: American

Text:

May 6, 1918 (Monday) – Very unfit with headache & cough. Rumor that we are to pull out. Orders later to same effect, followed by orders telling us we would relieve 149 Brigade. To bed early having taken 2 no. 9’s3 and 10 grams. Phenacetin.4
