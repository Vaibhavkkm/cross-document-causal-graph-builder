Last of refugees were sent away at 6:30 p.m. Then went & had dinner and prepared to get to bed. About 10 p.m. the Huns began shelling us so we left on train about 12 p.m. By this time shells were bursting all around us. Went thru Hazebrouck to Ebblinghem. Arrived 4 a.m. Had to sleep in hay loft with nothing but hay for cover.
