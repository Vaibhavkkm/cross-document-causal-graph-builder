
Author: Austen Chamberlain
Date: 20 July 1917
Location: Sussex
Nationality: British

Text:

TEL  TURNERS HILL 16.  TEL  ROWFANT STATION.*)  ROWFANT,  CRAWLEY,  SUSSEX.  20/7/1917.   My dear General  It was a real  pleasure to me to receive  your letter & very kind  of you to write. My   correspondence in these last   days has left me very  happy & not a little  proud of the possession  of the confidence & approval  of so many men whose   good opinion I value.  With all good wishes   to you   Yours sincerely,  Austen Chamberlain.
