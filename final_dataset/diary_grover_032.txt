Author: Grover Carter
Date: 14 April 1918
Rank: Lieutenant
Nationality: American

Text:

April 14, 1918 – Up at 7 a.m. Went out in search of unit and found it about 2 kilos from Abeele [Abele on today’s maps] Nice huts with plenty coal & stoves. Very glad to get back to my unit. Was thought to have been captured or killed. Very cold & windy.
