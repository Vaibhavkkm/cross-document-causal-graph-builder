Author: Frederick Harold Tubb

Date: Friday 04.06.1915.
Nationality: Australian.

Issued clothing to the men. Went in charge of the horses of our camp to swimming parade Capt Robin came too we had a grand swim the ocean is glorious! Received orders to shift our camp. We are to join in with the 3rd Coy A.S.C., Major Tedder will be the C.O: q  No further news of my going away.
