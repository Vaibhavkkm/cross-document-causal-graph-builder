Author: Grover Carter
Date: 27 March 1918
Rank: Lieutenant
Nationality: American

Text:

March 27, 1918 – Up at 8 a.m. Can hear big guns in the distance. Getting ready to move. Left 5:40 p.m. Marched 12 mi. Caught train 5 a.m. Petit Haravesnes?. Arrived at Merville 12:15 p.m. 5 mile march to Estaires. Very cold wind. Pretty well tired out. May have to leave out soon. Billeted about 1½ miles from mess. Pretty heavy firing about 8 or 10 miles. Many area lights seen. Slept well, between sheets for 1st time in France.
